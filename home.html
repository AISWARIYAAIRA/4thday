what is marriage?

Marriage, also called matrimony or wedlock is a culturally and often legally recognized union between people called spouses.
establishes rights and obligations between them, as well as between them and their children, and between them and their in-laws
It is considered a cultural universal, but the definition of marriage varies between cultures and religions, and over time.
Typically, it is an institution in which interpersonal relationships, usually sexual, are acknowledged or sanctioned.
In some cultures, marriage is recommended or considered to be compulsory before pursuing any sexual activity.
A marriage ceremony is called a wedding.
